Resistor value calculation (show your work)

Formula: R = (VCC − Vf) / I with VCC=5V. Example targets (edit to match your LEDs):

  • Red, Vf≈2.0 V, I≈10 mA ⇒ R≈(5−2.0)/0.01 ≈ 300 Ω → use 330 Ω or 220 Ω.
  • Green, Vf≈2.2 V, I≈10 mA ⇒ R≈(5−2.2)/0.01 ≈ 280 Ω → use 330 Ω or 270 Ω.
  • Blue, Vf≈3.0 V, I≈10 mA ⇒ R≈(5−3.0)/0.01 ≈ 200 Ω → use 220 Ω.
